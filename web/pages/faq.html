FAQ

Welcome to Maru Dry Fruits!

You can shop at Maru Dry Fruits either by registering with us or using a guest account to checkout. Creating an account on AlmondCo gives you access to the following benefits:

  • Manage your account details review past purchases

  • Save your shipping details for future purchases and a faster shopping experience

  • Stay up-to-date about our latest stock, special online promotions and discounts!

Orders can be placed while navigating our website as a registered user or even as a guest user. On any product page, select your item; choose the quantity you wish to purchase, and then press the “Add to Cart” button. This will open up your shopping cart where you may choose to continue shopping, or head on to checkout.

On the checkout page, you may add/confirm your personal and shipping information, select payment option, enter any discount vouchers (if applicable), and review your order before clicking on “Buy”.

We can accept payments through the following:

  1. COD(Cash on delivery)
  2. Bank Transfer / Bank Deposit
  3. Credit Card / MasterCard
  4. Jazz Cash

Shipping will vary city-to-city mostly on major cities we provide free shipping.

Delivery generally takes 2-5 business days in Pakistan and International orders are delivered in 8-12 days on average.

We take all measures to ensure that you receive your correct order. However, in case an item is missing or incorrect, you can get in touch with our Customer Support center to rectify this.

The parcel will be delivered by different courier services. Deliveries are made between 10.00am and 9:00pm.

We ensure that our website is updated regularly and that only the available items are listed. If a desired item is not available for a certain item, the product page will clearly identify “Out of Stock” for you. For queries and special requests concerning items and/or availability, please call or email our Customer Support center.

Our Customer Support Team will be happy to assist you in retrieving your account information. Please contact us via telephone or email to receive help on retrieving your account details.
